Best Movies of All Time According to James

My favourite movies

Hana-Bi

Directed by Takeshi Kitano

Beautifully shot drama about an ex cop trying to look after his disabled former partner and his sick wife.

Glengarry Glen Ross

Directed by James Foley

An all star cast make this movie an absolute must see. A group of salesmen in a small office in New York are put
under pressure when they find out that everyone but the top two salesmen of the month will be fired.

Pulp Fiction

Directed by Quentin Tarantino

No introduction needed.
